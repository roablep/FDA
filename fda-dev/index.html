What we do

We advocate for the common interests shared by the many commercial, retail, hospitality, and residential interests in our vibrant, mixed-use neighborhood. We provide a forum for neighbors to come together to discuss the challenges, opportunities and evolution of the neighborhood.

Community Engagement

Help neighbors build ongoing relationships, and productively engage to resolve concerns.

Civic Advocacy

Ensure that elected representatives and City agencies remain aware of the vital interests of our neighborhood.

Compliance

Seek accountability to the agreements, laws and regulations that protect the operations and vitality of the neighborhood.

Brand Building

Work to grow the neighborhood's distinct, strong, and authentic brand as the City's only continually-operating flower marketplace.
